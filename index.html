About us

Sphengecreations

Business origins

Sphengecreations was conceived out of a vision to bridge the gap between high-quality cleaning products and the specific needs of various customer segments in South Africa. We are a small but dynamic company driven by a passion for excellence in the cleaning supplies sector. Our journey began with a thorough understanding of market demands and the challenges faced by our target customer groups — insight that shapes our offerings and positions us to grow sustainably.

Competitive advantage

Our advantage lies in an unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Household supplies are designed to tackle a wide range of cleaning tasks for families seeking hygienic homes, while our industrial chemicals are engineered for the toughest grime in commercial settings — a reliable solution for cleaning services and hospitality professionals. By maintaining strong relationships, we continuously adapt and innovate to meet evolving needs.
